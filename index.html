Bill Splitter Application for COMP1073 final project

Project Overview

Creating an application that will allow you to upload a bill/invoice and select from a group of users to then split the bill/invoice. We will provide an interface where you can put users into categories for easy selection and will allow you to split the bill between all the members of a category. There will also be a simple static bill splitter with a tip generator for ease of use when youâ€™re out at a restaurant with friends!

Some Features Provided by this Application

  • Amount split equally
  • Add or calculate Tip based on total amount and split equally
  • Split Amount by proportion i.e. (1st person will pay 30% of total amount and other two will pay 35% of total amount).
  • Splitting Grocery Bills (Having some personal items and some items in common).
  • Split amount with Discount Amount.
  • Keep history of Data.
  • Able to create more than one Group.
  • Simple UI to navigate.
